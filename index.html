My Tasks

My Tasks is a personalized view of
every task that’s assigned to you,
regardless of which project it’s in.
Visualize all of your to-dos in one place
to ensure nothing falls through the cracks.

Time tracking

With native time tracking, quickly estimate
expected and actual time spent on tasks.
Get better real-time estimates on project
progress which is essential for budgeting
and forecasting.

Custom fields

Custom fields are the best way to tag,
sort, and filter work. Create unique
custom fields for any information you
need to track—from priority and status
to email or phone number.
